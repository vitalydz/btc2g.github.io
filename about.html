About BTC2G

BTC2G is a forecasting and educational platform visualizing the price evolution of Bitcoin and Gold — from 2016 through projections to 2030.

📊 Our Mission

To provide clear and unbiased visual insights comparing the world's oldest store of value (Gold) with the newest (Bitcoin). We aim to make data-driven projections accessible to everyone.

📈 Data & Forecasts

We use real-time financial data from trusted APIs like Yahoo Finance. Forecasts are based on polynomial trend analysis and presented in interactive charts.

🛑 Disclaimer

BTC2G is not a financial advisor. All content is for informational and educational purposes only. Please do your own research before making any investment decisions.
