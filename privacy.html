Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. BTC2G does not collect payment card data or run user accounts directly on this website. We use third-party services such as MoonPay, Topper, Stripe, Formspree, and Google Analytics, which may process data according to their own policies.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No user accounts or logins on BTC2G
  • No payment card data stored by BTC2G
  • No custom geolocation tracking by BTC2G

Third-Party Services

We use external providers like MoonPay, Topper, and Stripe for crypto purchase and subscription flows. These providers may collect data to facilitate transactions. Please refer to their privacy policies for full details.

Analytics

BTC2G uses Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate page views and button clicks. We do not send wallet addresses, payment card data, or email addresses to Google Analytics as custom event data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@btc2g.com
